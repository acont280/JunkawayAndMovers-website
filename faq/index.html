Junk Removal & Moving FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most from Bay Area customers.

How much does junk removal cost in the Bay Area? +
Pricing depends on the volume of junk and type of items being removed. We offer fair, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Call (415) 629-8055 for a free, no-obligation estimate.
Do you offer same-day junk removal? +
Yes! We offer same-day junk removal service throughout the San Francisco Bay Area, subject to availability. Give us a call to check today's schedule.
What items do you remove? +
We remove furniture, appliances, mattresses, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, and more. We also handle full property cleanouts for rentals, estates, and foreclosures. We sort items for recycling and donation whenever possible.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes, Junkaway & Movers LLC is fully licensed and insured for junk removal and moving services in California. Your belongings and property are protected.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve the entire San Francisco Bay Area including San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose, Berkeley, Fremont, Daly City, Hayward, Redwood City, Palo Alto, San Mateo, Sunnyvale, Santa Clara, and all surrounding communities.
Do you recycle or donate removed items? +
Absolutely. We're committed to eco-friendly disposal. We sort all items and donate usable goods to local charities and recycle materials whenever possible, keeping as much as we can out of landfills.
How quickly can you come out? +
We often do same-day or next-day jobs depending on availability. For urgent requests, give us a call and we'll do our best to accommodate your timeline. We're known for being fast and responsive.
Do I need to be home during the job? +
It's helpful but not always required. For junk removal, you can point out what needs to go and we'll handle the rest. For moving jobs, we recommend being present. Just let us know your situation and we'll work with you.
